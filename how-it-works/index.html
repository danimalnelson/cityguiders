How It Works

  • What is CityGuiders?

    CityGuiders helps people explore a new city with a personal concierge.

  • How does it work?

    Tell us when and where you are going and a little about you and your trip and we'll match you with someone that can help you find the perfect hotel, restaurants, activities, and anything else worth checking out.

  • People

    CityGuiders is a dedicated group of passionate locals that all share a common interest: showing off their city and helping people something.

  • How much does it cost?

    Nothing. CityGuiders is absolutely free.
